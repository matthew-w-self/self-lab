    <h2>Visual Processing</h2>
    <p>

    </p>

  <div class="research-box">
    <h2>Top-down Feedback</h2>
    <p>
      A major focus of the lab is understanding how higher brain areas influence sensory cortex through feedback and recurrent interactions.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="research-box">
    <h2>Prediction and Internal Models</h2>
    <p>
      We study how the brain uses prior knowledge and learned regularities to generate predictions about incoming sensory information.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="research-box">
    <h2>Neural Population Dynamics</h2>
    <p>
      We use modern recording and analysis techniques to examine how information is represented across populations of neurons.
    </p>
  </div>
